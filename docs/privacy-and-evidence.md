# Privacy and Evidence Notes

This portfolio is designed for public review without exposing private records.

## What is shown

- Sanitized, desaturated screenshots of real workflows.
- High-level metrics that were used in the Claude Corps resume.
- Architecture, ownership, and judgment notes for each project.

## What is intentionally withheld

- Personal email addresses, client names, and DM records.
- Private Notion database rows.
- Post URLs and deployment/account details.
- Private Driveline prescription details, access codes, and synced state.
- Shopify admin/theme IDs beyond generalized workflow evidence.

## Why this matters

Claude Corps fellows will work inside organizations with sensitive workflows and uneven AI maturity. The portfolio shows the work clearly while preserving the privacy boundary expected in nonprofits, schools, agencies, and public-interest settings.
